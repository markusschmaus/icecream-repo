Recipe

Fixed procedure, tunable composition. Edit a target and only the ingredient group responsible for it adjusts; identity ingredients and follower rules stay what they are.

Composition data are typical reference estimates, not lab measurements — edit the substance library below to match your ingredients.
% - churned volume

Targets

Each group of solver ingredients owns the targets it is responsible for. Edit a value — only that group's ingredients move (others shift minimally to keep their own targets pinned). The checkbox pins a metric: pinned values are held by the solver, unpinned ones are read-only outcomes. Not every combination can be pinned at once — dairy fat, MSNF, water and mass are interdependent, so pinning one means unpinning another.

Procedure

The steps are the fixed part of the recipe. Identity amounts are % of batch (creative decisions), followers track a rule, solver ingredients are set by the targets above.
